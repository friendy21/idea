Burnout Prophecy Engine

Predict burnout risk by analyzing subtle shifts in communication tone (e.g., increased passive voice, repetitive phrases) combined with calendar overload and overdue tasks.

Empathy Amplifier

Detect emotional exhaustion in messages and auto-suggest real-time coping strategies (e.g., "Your last 3 emails mentioned 'overwhelmed'—want to reschedule this deadline?").

Silent SOS

Flag phrases that hint at mental health struggles (e.g., "I can’t keep up") and anonymously connect employees with HR resources.

Flight Risk Radar

Identify "quiet quitters" by tracking declining communication volume, slower response times, and negative sentiment spikes, then trigger personalized retention offers.
